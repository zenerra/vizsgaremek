What is a UserID?

Your UserID (also referred to as aazon in our system) is a unique identifier assigned to you as an employee or authorized user. It is linked to your profile in our internal database and determines your access rights, including web access.


Steps to Obtain Your UserID

Check Your Employee Records:
Your UserID is typically provided when you join the company or when web access is granted. Check any welcome email, employee handbook, or HR documentation you received. Look for a number associated with your employee profile (e.g., 1, 2, etc.).
